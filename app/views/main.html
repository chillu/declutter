Welcome!

Haven't used that snowboard in years? Got a second TV gathering dust in the garage? Declutter helps you identify things you don't need.

Get up, walk around your place and take photos of stuff you haven't used in a while. You don't have to get rid of it just yet, Declutter will send you a reminder when it's time to decide, with some handy tips on simplifying your life.

Protip: Start with the big things!
